Welcome

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, specializing in Decision Support Systems, Entrepreneurial IS, Applied AI, and Generative AI. My research focuses on innovative applications of AI and data analytics in business and technology contexts.

Research Interests and Publications

My research spans various areas including decision support systems, entrepreneurial information systems, applied AI, and generative AI. Some of my notable publications include:

  • Exploring investors' expectancies and its impact on project funding success likelihood in crowdfunding by using text analytics and Bayesian networks (2022) - Decision Support Systems
  • Hierarchical balanced scorecard-based organizational goals and the efficiency of controls processes (2021) - Journal of Business Research
  • The Unobserved Heterogeneous Influence of Gamification and Novelty-Seeking Traits on Consumers' Repurchase Intention in the Omnichannel Retailing (2020) - Frontiers in Psychology

For a complete list of my publications, please visit my Google Scholar profile.
